FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything else you'd ask on the call — answered upfront. Still unsure? Ask us directly.

Ask a question
How much does a virtual assistant cost?+

Our managed virtual assistant services start at $7/hour for hourly support, $399/month for 40 hours, $549–$699/month part-time, and $999/month full-time (160 hours). Comparable US-based services typically run $2,400–$8,000/month. Every price is published on our pricing page.

Can I hire support hourly?+

Yes — hourly support starts at $7/hour for general VA and admin work, $8/hour for social media, CRM, customer support, and lead generation, $10/hour for design and video editing, and $12/hour for web development and automation. You pay only for time used, with weekly updates.

Do you offer fixed-price projects?+

Yes — websites, Shopify setups, WordPress fixes, CRM setup, automation workflows, landing pages, and design packages are delivered as scoped, fixed-price projects. Example starting ranges: landing pages from $299, business websites from $699, Shopify setup from $799, CRM setup from $750, starter automation from $750.

Can I interview the team member before hiring?+

Yes — we shortlist 1–2 candidates from our vetted team, you interview them, you approve, and only then does billing start. If it's ever not the right fit, we replace them free.

Do you work with US, UK, Canada, Australia, and UAE clients?+

Yes — most of our 1,000+ clients are in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and UAE. We support each market with agreed timezone overlap and one simple monthly invoice — no payroll or compliance overhead.

Can your team work in my timezone?+

Whether you need EST, CST, PST, UK, Gulf, or Australian timezone overlap, we agree on the working hours before your plan starts — so you always know when your team is online.

What if I am not satisfied with the assigned person?+

Your first week is risk-free — if you're not satisfied, we adjust, replace, or pause before continuing. After that, you get a free replacement anytime and month-to-month flexibility.

Do you offer monthly packages?+

Yes — every plan is month-to-month, from $399/month Starter Support to custom Managed Growth Teams from $1,499/month. Pause, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel monthly.

Can you handle multiple services together?+

Yes — that's our Managed Growth Team: VA and admin support, social media, design, web, CRM, lead generation, and automation under one account manager, from $1,499/month.

Is Sparking Asia better than hiring freelancers directly?+

For ongoing, business-critical work — yes. You get a managed team with account management, quality checks, free replacement, and a verifiable Upwork track record, instead of depending on one person's availability. For a small one-off task with no deadline pressure, a good freelancer can be enough — and we'll tell you that honestly on the call.
