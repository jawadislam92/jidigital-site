Make.com Automation FAQs

Zapier or Make — which should we use?+

Zapier for speed and simple app-to-app triggers; Make for complex multi-step logic, heavy data transformation, and cost efficiency at volume. We build on both and will recommend honestly on the call.

Can you migrate our existing Zaps to Make?+

Yes — we map your current workflows, rebuild them as Make scenarios with proper error handling, and run both in parallel before switching over.
