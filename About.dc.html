Ji Digital Works — powered by our Upwork agency Sparking Asia — is a managed offshore growth team based in Rawalpindi, Pakistan, serving international businesses across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and UAE.

The story

From hands-on freelancer to managed remote team

Ji Digital Works was built by Junaid Islam, a Pakistan-based founder who grew from hands-on freelancing into a managed remote team serving international clients. After years of working with global businesses through Upwork and remote operations, Junaid saw the same problem again and again:

Businesses didn't just need freelancers — they needed a reliable, managed team that could handle admin, design, social media, web, CRM, lead generation, and automation under one roof.

That team became Sparking Asia on Upwork — and Ji Digital Works for clients who work with us directly. Same people, same standards, same accountable delivery.
