Privacy Policy — summary

We collect only the information you give us through forms and calls — name, work email, company details, and what you need help with — and use it solely to respond, scope your plan, and deliver services.

Client work is handled under NDA by default, through managed accounts with controlled, revocable access. We never sell client data, never reuse client assets, and remove access immediately when an engagement ends.

Analytics on this site is limited to privacy-respecting, aggregate usage measurement. A detailed security one-pager is available on request at hello@jidigitalworks.com.

Terms of Service — summary

Monthly plans are month-to-month: you can pause, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at each renewal. Hourly support is billed for time used with weekly updates. Fixed-price projects are scoped, quoted, and delivered against agreed milestones.

Every engagement includes our guarantees: interview before you pay, a 1-week risk-free start, and free replacement of team members at any time. We are your vendor — one invoice, no payroll or employment obligations on your side.

You own everything we produce for you, including source files. Final pricing depends on role, workload, timezone coverage, tools, and scope, and is confirmed in writing before billing starts.
